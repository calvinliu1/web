      How do you make a sandwich?

      Making a sandwich isn't too hard. You simply take two pieces of bread and toast it (via toaster), if you want to. Take the toasted or non-toasted bread and lay them both down. Take your ingredients and layer them ontop one slice of bread. After you are satisifed with what or how much you put on top of the bread, put the second slice on top of the ingredients.

      What should you eat with a sanwich?

      A sandwich should never be eaten alone. A sandwich should always be eaten with a side of chips or a drink. Chips simply because of the texture it offers. A change of texture when eating a sandwich can amplify the taste of the sandwich. A drink simply because a drink is good for anything.
